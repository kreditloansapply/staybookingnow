FAQs

1. What amenities are included in the Deluxe Room?

The Deluxe Room includes high-speed Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, a minibar, premium toiletries, and plush bedding for a comfortable stay.

Yes, complimentary breakfast is included with your Deluxe Room booking.

Many Deluxe Rooms offer scenic views; availability may vary based on location within the hotel.
